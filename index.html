About Me

Hello and welcome to my online portfolio. My name is Lois, aged 26 and recently undertook the EdX Front End Development course with an aim to break into the tech industry. As an ex Teacher, recently turned Employment Advisor, I have always been interested in coding but was unsure how to proceed. After completing a few beginners courses at university and online, I came across the bootcamp which I am hopeful will be the perfect stepping stone to finally becoming a front end designer. Please browse my site to check our my recent work, social media and contact details. Please follow along on my journey to becoming a coding expert and I hope to hear from you soon!
